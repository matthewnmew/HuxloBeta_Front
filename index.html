SUPER SUSTAINABLE.

We believe sustainable building design should be the norm, so we're going to make it that way. All of our parts are manufactured from fast growing sustainable softwoods, ensuring that we leave behind enough resources for generations to come. Our precision manufacturing technique results in an almost air-tight structure, this ensures low energy loss and therefore lower heating bills.

ENGINEERED TO LAST.

Our team of engineers have done the hard bit. We've designed a range of structurally sound parts that fit together using our unique joinery system. With the engineering already done, you're free to design your space on aesthetics and a layout that suits you best.

COMMUNITY DRIVEN.

Here you can search through other designs creations for inspiration or as a building block for your dream space. The 3D design environment makes both making and viewing your creations a simple, pleasant experience.

COMMUNITY EMPOWERMENT.

Our aim is to empower individuals to create they're own spaces by lowering the barrier to entry of good design. We believe aesthetic, sustainable design should be in the reaches of everyone.
